Introduction

Hello, I'm here to tell you all a little about myself. My name is Christopher Ou(pronounced oh), and I'm 32 years old. I've been interested in a lot of things since I was a child. The hobbies included are sports, cars, animals, science, computers, games, and much more. Please explore this webpage and I hope that you can get to know me better.

about

As I mentioned before my name is Christopher but I prefer to be called Chris and my close friends call me "Domo". I am passionate with a lot of things and I recently stumbled upon programming. The thrill of completing a line of code that has been nothing but trouble, the satisfaction of seeing all your "mumbo-jumbo" turn into a beautiful webpage, and the comradery of working with many other skilled and talented developers is what draws me to this field.

My interest for programming started very recently. I've noticed more and more people going into this field and I was hooked. Taking a look at some YouTube videos and doing freeCodeCamp was the first time I got my feet wet. Now I am diving into the deep end and I want to show what I've been working on. Before that, I want to highlight some hobbies that have shaped me into the person I am today.
